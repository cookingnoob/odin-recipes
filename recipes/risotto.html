Caramelized Carrot Risotto

Creamy Risotto with Caramelized Carrot Purée

In this recipe you are going to learn to cook risotto. The best part is you're not going to be stirring a pot for eternity. It is quite easy and you'll enjoy a restaurant quality meal at home with your loved ones or by yourself.

Ingridients

  • Caramelized Carrot Purée
    • 500g carrots, peeled and diced
    • 113g butter
    • 30g water
    • 5g salt
    • 2.5g baking soda
  • Risotto
    • 1 1/2 cups arborio rice
    • 4 cups carrot juice (or chicken stock)
    • 1 cup white wine
    • 2 tsp butter
    • 2 tbs olive oil
    • 2 garlic gloved, finely chopped
    • 2 shallots, finely chopped
    • 3/4 cups Caramelized Carrot Purée
    • 85g parmesan cheese, grated
    • salt
  • Sautéed mushrooms
    • 500g portobello mushrooms, washed and diced
    • 1 tsb olive oil
    • 1 tsb butter
    • 1 clove of garlic, finely chopped
    • 1/4 cup white wine

Steps

  • Caramelized Carrot Purée
    1. Melt the butter on a pressure cooker
    2. Add the carrot, salt, water and bakind soda, mix well
    3. Cook under pressure for 20 minutes
    4. Depressurise the pot
    5. Add the ingredientes to a food processor and blend until smooth
    6. Reserve hot until risotto is done
  • Risoto
    1. On a 12 inch sautée pan melt the butter and olive oil
    2. Once the butter is bubbling add the shallots, garlic and rice. Toast until fragant, about 3 minutes
    3. Add the wine and let it reduce to half
    4. Add a third of the juice and put a lid on the pan. Let it cook for 10 minutes on medium heat
    5. Stir the rice, add another third of the juice, put the lid on and let cook for other 10 minutes.
    6. Add the last third of the stock and keep stirring until the liquid has become a creamy sauce. It takes around 8 minutes.
    7. Once the liquid is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon add the carrot purée, parmesan cheese and most of the mushrooms, mix it well
    8. Serve inmidiatly on a hot plate. garnish with the rest of mushrooms
  • Sautéed mushrooms
    1. Heat a pan with olive oil and butter
    2. Once its bubbling add the mushrooms and salt. Cook them until they lost half their size and are golden brown
    3. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant
    4. Add the white wine and cook until it reduced completely and there is a fine shiny layer on the mushrooms
    5. Keep it warm until the final step of the risotto
