Carbonara

Creamy and delicious carbonara thats easy to make at home!

Did you know carbonara doesnt need cream or garlic? Yeah, thats right, traditional carbonara gets creamy with a secret ingredient that people normally throw away at the sink

You'll enjoy a restaurant quality meal that is so easy to make and it only takes 30 minutes to complete.

Ingridients

  • 200g of dry pasta
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 200g pancetta (in a pinch you can use bacon)
  • 150g parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water on a pot and add enough salt that it tastes salty
  2. Cook the pasta as the package instructs
  3. Save the pasta in cool water, keep the water where you cooked the pasta
  4. Cook on low heat the pancetta with the wine until is golden brown
  5. Mix the eggs, parmesan cheese and pepper (a ton of pepper if you like it)
  6. On low heat add the pasta to the pancetta pan, on top of that the egg mixture and a big spoon of pasta water, mix vigorously wiht a pair of tongs
  7. The sauce must be creamy and silky, if it is lumpy add more pasta water, it will emulsify the sauce so dont be shy adding it
  8. serve inmediatly, garnish with more pepper and enjoy!
