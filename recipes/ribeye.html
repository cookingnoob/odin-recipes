Ribeye with Pomme Purée

Treat yourself with a steakhouse dinner at home

This is one of the best treat myself dishes out there and you can make it

Its so delicious and you can share it with a loved one!

Ingridients

  • Ribeye Steak
    • 500-700g Ribeye Steak
    • Salt
    • Pepper
  • Pomme Purée
    • 750g potatoes
    • 225g cold butter, cubed
    • 190g whole cream
    • 50g clarified butter (optional)
    • Salt
    • Pepper

    Steps

    • For the Ribeye
      1. Salt generously the steak on all sides. Let it rest at leat 1 hour in a baking rack, up to 3 days in the fridge
      2. Preheat your oven to 200F
      3. Cook the meat until it its around 5F bellow the desired temp
        1. medium rare 126F
        2. medium 134F
        3. well done 160F I dont recommend this term for this kind of steak
      4. On a high heat pan put some highsmoke point oil like canola, once its smoking sear the steak, 3 minutes per side
      5. Let it rest for 15 minutes before cutting it
      6. Put a generous dollop of pomme purée bellow and crown it with the beautiful steak, enjoy!
    • For the pomme purée
      1. Fill with water a pot, put the potatoes and cook them until tender
      2. With a utility knife peele the potatoes, keep them warm
      3. Use a potato ricer to process them, if you want a finer texture you can use a tamis
      4. Add the potatoes to a pot and incorporate 1/3 of the whole cream and some cubes of butter until they are emulsified. Keep adding until it has your desired consistenscy
      5. Add salt and pepper to taste
      6. You're done, enjoy!
